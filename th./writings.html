Love is an interesting feeling: So permanent and fleeting. So vague and so uplifting with no concept of timing. I’d love to one day be a wife but I cannot comprehend the thought of life with just one. Many reasons why love turns to goodbye. My heart screams and cries in fear of losing this one. Don’t know what I’d do without this one. But the thought of living happily in love seems so illogical. How could one yearn continual? What I know for sure is what I’m feeling right now is the surest thing I’ve ever known. But emotions flow straight from high to low. The things you know sometimes change. It’s so strange. Things change everyday and I need this right here to stay. The best bet is to take it one day at a time. Let things fall as they rise. Let us enjoy the ride.
