What is Fairy Good Cook?

A personal chef service offering delicious meals made to order by a chef with 10 years of experience in the New York Food Industry.

Who is Fairy Good Cook?

Hello, I'm Chef Nilda Rodriguez!

I am a native New Yorker who specializes in Puerto Rican and Classic American Cuisine.

  My Roots

As a young girl, I learned traditional Puerto Rican cooking.

Growing up, I also appreciated the diversity of New York City and it is surely reflected in my cooking.

  Career Highlights

I began my cooking career working in New York City restaurants, most recently at Butter Midtown where Celebrity Chef Alex Guarnaschelli serves as Executive Chef.

While at Butter, I learned the importance of utilizing the freshest and finest seasonal vegetables and ingredients.

  What I Value

I value everyday home cooking. My dishes are family friendly, nourishing, and comforting.

You will enjoy simple but delicious homemade meals and desserts with a bit of a twist.
