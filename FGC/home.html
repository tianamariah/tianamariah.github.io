Who is Fairy Good Cook?

Chef Nilda Rodriguez is a native New Yorker who specializes in Puerto Rican and Classic American Cuisine.

  My Roots

As a young girl, Nilda learned traditional Puerto Rican cooking but while growing up she appreciated the diversity of New York City and it is reflected in her cooking.

  Career Highlights

She began her cooking career working in New York City restaurants, most recently at Butter Midtown where Celebrity Chef Alex Guarnaschelli serves as Executive Chef. While at Butter Nilda learned the importance of utilizing the freshest and finest seasonal vegetables and ingredients.

  What I Value

Nilda values everyday home cooking. Her dishes are family friendly, nourishing, and comforting. You will enjoy simple but delicious homemade meals and desserts with a bit of a twist.
